The idea of this activity is for the Scouts to think about what things they have used today, which are new, what they did with them and the packaging of them. The idea of this activity is to highlight the number of things that people use each day and the impact of these items.

You’ll Need

  • Pens & Paper or,
  • Access to Slido/Digital Resources or,
  • A dry erase board and pens

How to Play

  1. First start of my getting all the Scouts to think of all the things they’ve used today. It could be bread for their sandwich, their toothbrush, a chocolate bar, etc. Get them to take a few minutes to think of a list of these things.
  1. If not using Slido: come together as a group, go round one by one asking each person for a new item that they used. Write the items into a master list on a large piece of paper or a board. In all cases: Review the items that have been used, was there any strange items? Any items that were missing, if so you can add them?
  1. Now get the Scouts to think about the packaging and waste that has been generated by using these items today. Some items may have been unpackaged before today and some may have been unpackaged today. Give them a few minutes to think of things again before coming together.
  1. If not using Slido: come together as a group, go round one by one asking each person for a new item of packaging and waste from an item that they used. Write the items into a master list on a large piece of paper or a board. In all cases: Review the items the list of packaging that has been created. Again were any strange items? Any items that were missing, if so you can add them?
  1. Finally get the Scouts to think about what they did with the packaging, go through the items of packaging/waste one by one and ask what the Scouts did with the waste? Did they do the right thing with it for your local recycling/waste disposal system?
  1. Once you’ve gone though most of the items ask the Scouts if there was any items that they were surprised they could/couldn’t recycle?

Using Sildo for this Activity

Slido is a free interactive online tool that can be used for this activity as an alternative. For this you’ll need a internet connection, a laptop/computer, projector or TV to display answers on and a free Slido account.

Once you’ve got an account you need to create a new “Slido” event, within the event add two questions using the Word Cloud option:

  1. What things have you used today?
  1. What packaging and waste was created by using your items?

When your ready click the Present button in the top right, information about joining and a QR code will appear on Screen. Have the Scouts scan the QR code or use the link to join in.

Once all have joined hover over the bottom left of the screen and click the “play” icon. This will make the first question appear and allow the Scouts to answer it. If more than one person adds the same item it will appear larger on screen.

Click the “lock” icon in the bottom bar to stop allowing items to be added. Discuss the answers before moving on.

Once you’ve finished you can click the “stop” icon in the bottom left to stop the poll (ℹ️ the answers will disappear from your screen) or press the “fast forward” icon to move to the next question.
